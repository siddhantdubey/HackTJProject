Our Mission

Sometimes, while we're in our cars, waiting at the stop light, we see that an, holding a cardborad poster, pleading for a little bit of help. just a little help. Or that woman who lives on the streets who can't afford a new pair of shoes because she has to feed her children. At A Little Gift, you can help your brothers and sisters in need. Maybe give then that new blanket they need to last the winter. Or that new pair of shoes. We believe that if you can give a little gift, you can make a big difference.
